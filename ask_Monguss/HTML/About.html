About ask_Mongüss

ask_Mongüss is an educational tool designed by students and instructors, for students and instructors, to help facilitate discussion and learning outside the normal classroom environment. This application is one more tool in the educator's toolbox to provide a quality education that will last a student's lifetime.

ask_Mongüss allows students to ask their instructors questions about their course's syllabus, assignments, and exams, and provides a Frequently Asked Questions page that instructors can customize to the needs of their students. This not only centralizes questions regarding course content into a single source, but also frees up the instructors e-mails so that they can better organize their tasks.
